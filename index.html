Xstylz

Early Life

Hamilton Chinanu Nwankwo, popularly known as XSTYLZ, was born into the Nwankwo Family of Uhumgbeke in Mkpodabo, Ohambele, Ndoki, Ukwa East LGA, Abia state. Raised in Eziama, Aba, Abia state.

He attended Eziama Primary school and Eziama high school before progressing to Boys Technical College, Osusu Aba, and later Abia State Polytechnic.

Musical Journey

XSTYLZ discovered his passion for music during his secondary school days, where he started writing songs. After completing secondary school, he formed a crew named HHS (Hamzy, Hella, Spurn).

The crew, with Hella as his closest friend and Spurn met at PZ Industries in Aba where they worked together. The trio had a collection of audio songs and were in the process of planning an album release when Spurn got a visa to South Africa and relocated.

In 2014, XSTYLZ and Hella moved to Istanbul. During their first year in the vibrant city, they performed at the Afro Istanbul club, the only African club in Istanbul at the time, gaining popularity.



Musical Influences

Growing up, XSTYLZ developed a love for Hip-hop, R&B, and Afromusic, drawing inspiration from artists such as Biggie, JayZ, Nas, Wu Tang Clan, Mobb Deep, Jagged Edge, 112, R Kelly, Tony Braxton, 2Baba, Remedies, Mode9, VIP, and more.

Presently, his key influences and role models include Burna Boy and 6lack.

Musical Style & Latest Release

XSTYLZ's musical style can be described as a fusion of Chill Vibe, Afro, and Rap, showcasing a unique blend of cultural influences and contemporary sounds.

XSTYLZ's latest effort, the single titled "DO IT FOR LOVE," was released on January 27, 2024. The track is available on all online platforms.

Vision

XSTYLZ is on a mission to contribute to the global music scene by creating a distinct sound that resonates with listeners, blending his cultural roots with contemporary influences.

With Burna Boy and 6lack as his guiding lights, XSTYLZ aims to make an impact in the Afro/Rap genre and continue evolving as an artist.
