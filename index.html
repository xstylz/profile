Meet XSTYLZ

Hamilton Chinanu Nwanko a.k.a XSTYLZ was born in the South Eastern part of Nigeria. XSTYLZ draws inspiration from the vibrant culture and rich heritage of the region.

He hails from the family of eight, as the son of the Late Ichie Friday Nwankwo of Uhumgbeke in Mkpodabo, Ohambele, Ndoki, Ukwa East LGA, Abia state. Raised in the bustling city of Eziama Aba in Abia state, he was immersed in the sights, sounds, and traditions of his homeland from an early age.



XSTYLZ pursued his education at Abia State Polytechnic in Aba, where he honed his artistic skills and laid the foundation for his future endeavors in the music industry before traveling to Istanbul, Turkey.

Specializing in the art of Afrobeat and Rap expression, XSTYLZ captivates audiences with his unique blend of lyricism, rhythm, and storytelling. His music serves as a reflection of his life experiences, cultural roots, and aspirations, resonating with listeners on a deeply personal level.

XSTYLZ is currently working on his next single (TROBU), promising an electrifying fusion of sound and substance that will leave a lasting impression on audiences far and wide. "Do It For Me" being the first single released off his upcoming album, getting heavy rotations in the clubs and on airwaves. He has performed alongside great music acts within Africa and beyond.
